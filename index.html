Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between SonicWave Pro and SonicWave Pro Ultra?

The SonicWave Pro Ultra features upgraded SonicPulse™ drivers, Adaptive ANC Pro, Bluetooth 5.3, Spatial Audio, longer battery life, and premium materials for an enhanced audio experience.

Are these headphones compatible with gaming consoles?

Yes, they are compatible with devices that support Bluetooth audio, including some gaming consoles. For consoles without Bluetooth, you can use a Bluetooth adapter.

Can I use these headphones for making calls?

Yes, the SonicWave Pro Ultra comes with a CrystalClear microphone, ensuring clear and crisp calls.

How do I activate Spatial Audio?

Spatial Audio can be activated through the SonicWave Pro app on your mobile device. Ensure your audio source supports Spatial Audio.

What is the range of the Bluetooth connection?

The Bluetooth 5.3 provides a stable connection up to 33 feet (10 meters) in ideal conditions.

Are the ear cups replaceable?

Yes, the ear cups are designed to be replaceable. Replacement ear cups can be purchased from our website.

Does it have a wired option?

Yes, it includes a 3.5mm audio cable for wired listening.
